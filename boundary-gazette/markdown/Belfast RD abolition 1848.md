IN THE PARLIAMENT OF NORTHERN IRELAND, SESSION
1956-7
NEWTOWNABBEY URBAN DISTRICT
(To constitute the Belfast Rural District an Urban
District; to change the name of the said dis-
trict; to alter the boundaries of the said district
and the boundaries of the Rural Districts of
Antrim, Lisburn and Larne; to confer further
powers upon the Council of the said Urban Dis-
trict: and for other purposes.)
NOTICE is hereby given that the Chairman and
Councillors of the Rural District of Belfast (herein-
after referred to as "the Council") intend to apply
to Parliament in the ensuing Session for leave to
bring in a Bill (hereinafter referred to as "the
Bill") of which the following are the main objects.
FORMATION OF NEWTOWNABBEY URBAN DISTRICT,
ABOLITION 
OF BELFAST 
RURAL DISTRICT AND
ALTERATION OF BOUNDARIES OF EXISTING RURAL
DISTRICTS OF ANTRIM, 
LISBURN 
AND LARNE
To provide for the formation of a new Urban Dis-
trict which shall consist of the townlands of
Ballyaghagan, Bally duff, Ballygolan, Carnmoney,
Carnmoney Bog, Carnmoney Glebe, Collinward,
Croghfern, Drumnadrough, Dunanney, Glen-
gormley, Whiteabbey and Whitehouse, together
with parts of the townlands of Ballybought,
Ballyhenry, 
Ballyhowne, 
Jordanstown 
and
Monkstown.
To provide that the Urban District shall be called
Newtown abbey Urban District and shall be
under the control of a new Urban District
Council which shall have all the functions that
attach by virtue of any enactment to urban
district councils generally (which functions in-
clude the power to levy tolls, rates or duties, or
to alter any existing tolls, rates or duties, or to
confer, vary or extinguish any exemption from
payment of tolls, rates or duties, or to confer,
vary or extinguish any other rights or privi-
leges, or to impose on any lands or houses, or
to render any lands or houses liable to the
imposition of any charge in respect of any im-
provement insofar as such powers are conferred
on the new Urban District Council by virtue
of any enactment that applies to urban districts
and to urban district councils generally together
with such further powers to act in the aforesaid
manner as are hereunder set out.)
To provide for the abolition of Belfast Rural
District and the dissolution of Belfast Rural
District Council.
To provide for the extension of the boundaries of
Antrim Rural District, Lisburn Rural District
and Larne Rural District by the addition of the
following areas to the said Rural districts
respectively:
(a) to Antrim Rural District the town-
lands of

said areas.

ception of certified copies of, or extracts from,
