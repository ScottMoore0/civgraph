THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT ACTS (N.I.), 1898-1962,
AND THE URBAN DISTRICTS BOUNDARIES
(PROCEDURE) ORDER, 1936
Order of the County Council of Antrim altering
and extending the Boundaries of the said
Urban County and Rural Districts
Whereas, on the 23rd day of February, 1965, an
application was made by the Council of the said
Urban County District of Newtownabbey (herein-
after referred to as "the said Urban Council") to
the County Council of the said County, pursuant to
the provisions of Section 24 of the Local Government
Act (Northern Ireland), 1934, for the alteration and
extension of the existing boundaries of the said
Urban County and Rural Districts.
And whereas, the said County Council of the said
County being satisfied that a prima facie case had
been made out for the proposal to alter the existing
boundaries of the said Urban County and Rural
Districts and pursuant to the said Act and to the
said Order, appointed William S. Henderson, F.C.A.,
to hold such Inquiry as authorised and prescribed
by the said Act and Order.
And whereas, the said William S. Henderson,
F.C.A., duly held such Local Inquiry (due notice of
same and of the purport thereof having been pre-
viously given in compliance with the requirements
of the said Order), at the Town Hall, Newtownabbey,
in'the said County, on Thursday, the 29th December,
1966.
And whereas, the said William S. Henderson,
F.C.A., duly made his report of such Inquiry to the
said County Council at a meeting thereof held on
the 25th day of April, 1967.
And whereas, the said County Council, on con-
sideration of said Report at that meeting did adopt
the same, and decided to make an Order embodying
the terms of said Report for submission to the
Ministry of Development.
Now therefore We, the said Council of the said
Administrative County of Antrim, in pursuance of
the powers conferred upon us by the .said Section
24 of the Local Government Act (N.I.), 1934, and of
all other powers Us thereunto enabling do hereby
Order as follows:
(1) That the Boundary of the said Urban County
District of Newtownabbey be altered and extended
by including within the said Urban County District,
the area described in the schedule hereto, and more
particularly delineated on the map this day sub-
mitted to and adopted by Us and signed by our
Chairman and Secretary, whereon so much of the
said extended area is coloured Pink, and by exclud-
ing the area described in the schedule hereto and
more particularly delineated on the said map where-
on so much of the said excluded area is coloured
Blue.
(2) That the Boundary of the said Rural District
of Antrim be altered and extended by including
within the said Rural District, the area described in
the schedule hereto, and more particularly delineated
on the map this day submitted to and adopted by
Us and signed by our Chairman and Secretary,
whereon so much of the said extended area is
coloured Blue, and by excluding the area described
in the schedule hereto and more particularly de-
lineated on the said map whereon so much of the
said excluded area is coloured Pink.
(3) That the financial adjustments consequent upon
the making of this Order be the subject of an Agree-
ment to be entered into by the parties concerned,
such Agreement to be approved by the Ministry of
Development for Northern Ireland, and in the absence
of Agreement between the parties, to be determined
by the Ministry.
(4) And that the said Area coloured Pink, do form
part of the said Urban County District of New-
townabbey and that the said Area coloured Blue, do
form part of the Rural District of Antrim for the
purposes of the Electoral Law Act (Northern Ire-
land) 1962 from the 15th day of February, 1968, and
for all other purposes from the 1st day of April,
1968.
(5) That the area included within the Urban
County District of Newtownabbey described in the
Schedule and coloured Pink on the map should be
added to the District of supply of Belfast City and
District Water Commissioners.
(6) That the area included in the Rural District of
Antrim described in the Schedule and coloured Blue
on the map should be removed from the District of
supply of Belfast City and District Water Com-
missioners.
Sealed with the Seal of the said County Council
at a meeting thereof held this 12th day of
December, 1967.
