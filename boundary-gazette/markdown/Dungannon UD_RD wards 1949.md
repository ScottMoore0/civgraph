MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND LOCAL
GOVERNMENT FOR NORTHERN IRELAND
RURAL DISTRICT OF DUNGANNON
To the Council of the Administrative County of
Tyrone;
To the Council of the Rural District of Dungannon;
and
To all others whom it may concern.
WHEREAS the Council of the Urban District of
Dungannon, on the Hth day of July, 1947, submitted
a proposal to the Council of the Administrative
County of Tyrone (in this Order referred to as the
"County Council") for the alteration of the boundary
of the said urban district:
And WHEREAS the County Council, in exercise
of the powers conferred upon them by section twenty-
four of the Local Government Act (Northern Ireland),
1934, and of all other powers thereunto them enabling,
at a quarterly meeting held on the 15th day of Dec-
ember, 1947, made an Order altering the boundary
of the said urban district:
And WHEREAS the Council of the Urban District
of Dungannon appealed to the Ministry of Health
and Local Government for Northern Ireland (in this
Order referred to as "the Ministry") against the said
Order:
And WHEREAS the Ministry on the 26th day of
August, 1948, made an Order altering the boundary
of the said urban district:
And WHEREAS the Rural District of Dungannon,
situate in the Administrative County of Tyrone, is
now divided into nineteen District Electoral Divisions
named respectively the Aghnahoe, Altmore, Benburb,
Bernagh, Bush, Caledon, Castlecaulfield, Clonaneese,
Clonavaddy, Coalisland, Crossdernot, Donaghmore,
Granville, Killyman, Meenagh, Minterburn, Mount-
joy, Moy and Newmills District Electoral Divisions:
And WHEREAS it is expedient that the con-
stitution of the District Electoral Divisions of Ber-
nagh, Bush, Granville and Newmills should be altered
in the manner hereinafter appearing:
NOW, THEREFORE, the Ministry, in exercise of
its powers under the Poor Relief Acts (Northern
Ireland), 1838 to 1947, the Local Government Acts
(Northern Ireland), 1898 to 1948, and all other powers
in that behalf enabling the Ministry, hereby orders
and directs that: —
(1) This Order shall take effect as and from the
first day of April, 1949;
(2) Notwithstanding anything contained in any
Order to the contrary the District Electoral
Divisions of Bernagh, Bush, Granville and New-
mills, in the said Rural District, shall consist
of the townlands, parts of townlands and places
set forth by name in Column 2 of the Schedule
to this Order opposite to the name of the said
district electoral division.
AND it is hereby declared that the foregoing pro-
visions of this Order shall in no way affect or be
deemed to affect the limits of any area heretofore
determined by an Order to be the area of charge
upon which any special expenses as defined by Sec-
tion 232 of the Public Health (Ireland) Act, 1878,
or any other expenses directed by any Act to be
defrayed as such special expenses are and shall be
chargeable.
Sealed with the Official Seal of the
Ministry of Health and Local Gov-
ernment for Northern Ireland this
fourth day of October, one thousand,
nine hundred and forty-eight, in the
presence of
THOS. ELWOOD,
Assistant Secretary.
