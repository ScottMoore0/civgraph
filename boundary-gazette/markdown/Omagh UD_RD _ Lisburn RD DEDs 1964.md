Whereas on the 8th day of April, 1963, an applica-
tion was made by the Council of the said Urban
County District of Omagh (hereinafter referred to as
the said "Urban Council") to the County Council of
the County of Tyrone (hereinafter called the "County
Council") pursuant to the provisions of .Section 24 of
the Local Government Act (N.I.), 1934, for the alter-
ation and extension of the existing boundary of the
said Urban County District.
And Whereas the said County Council being satis-
fied that a prima facie case had been made out for
the proposal to alter the existing boundary of the
said Urban County District by extending same, and
pursuant to the said Act, and to the said Order, by
Resolution dated 8th day of April, 1963, appointed
W. H. Smith, Esq., LL.iB., W. S. Rea, Esq., B.A.,
M.iB.E., and William J. Purdy, Esq., being members
of the County Council, to hold such Local Inquiry as
authorised and prescribed by the said Act and Order.
And Whereas the said Messrs. Smith, Rea and
Purdy held such Local Inquiry (due notice of same
and of the purport thereof having been previously
given in compliance with the requirements of the
said Order) at the County Hall, Omagh, in the said
County, on Thursday, 9th day of May, 1963.
And Whereas the said Messrs. Smith, Rea and
Purdy duly made their report of such Inquiry to the
County Council at the Adjourned Quarterly Meeting
thereof, held on the 17th day of June, 1963.
And Whereas the County Council on consideration
of said Report at said meeting held on the 17th day
of June, 1963, did adopt the same and directed that
steps be taken to have an Order prepared and made
embodying the terms of said report for submission
to the Ministry of Health and Local Government
for Northern Ireland.
Now, Therefore, We the County Council of the
said Administrative County of Tyrone in pursuance
of the powers conferred upon us by the said Section
24 of the Local Government Act (Northern Ireland),
1934, and all other powers us thereunto enabling do
hereby order as follows: —
1. The adjustment or award made by J. W. Dniry,
Esq., and bearing date the 26th day of September,
1901 in so far as it determines the financial relations
between the Administrative County of Tyrone and
the Urban .County District of Omagh shall apply and
have effect in like manner as if the Urban County
District of Omagh therein mentioned were the Urban
County District as extended by this Order when con-
firmed by the Ministry of Health and Local Govern-
ment.
2. That the boundary of the Urban County 'Dis-
trict of Omagh be altered by including within the
said Urban County District the area described in ^the
schedule hereto and more particularly delineated on
the map annexed hereto and edged pink.
3. That the financial adjustments consequent upon
the making of this Order shall be the subject of an
Agreement to be entered into by the parties con-
cerned, such agreement to be approved of by the
Ministry, and in the absence of agreement between
the parties to be determined by the Ministry.
4. That every person holding office on the 1st
day of April, 1964, as County Councillor or District
Councillor for any area affected by the Order, and
every County Councillor or District Councillor
elected for any such area before the 1st day of
April, 1964, shall be deemed to have been elected
for the area as altered by the Order, and the County
Council or District Council, of which he is a member,
shall be deemed to have been elected for the County
District as altered by the Order.
5. That the Bye Laws at present in force in the
said Urban District shall apply to the area as altered
by the Order.
6. That the local and adopted Acts and local
Orders in force in the said Unban County District
shall apply to the area as altered by the Order,
7. That notwithstanding the alteration of the
boundary of the said Urban County District by the
Order, the said County Council shall have power to
recover arrears of rates in the area affected.
8. The said altered area do form the said Urban
County District of Omagh for the purpose of the
Electoral Law Act. 1962, as from 1st September,
1963 and for all other purposes as from 1st April,
1964.
9. This Order shall be cited as the Omagh Urban
District (Alteration) Order, 1963.

To the Council of the Urban District of Omagh; anti

Whereas the Council of the Administrative 'County
of Tyrone at a meeting held on the 17th June, 1963,
made the Omagh Urban District (Alteration) Order,
1963, altering the boundary of the Urban District of
Omagh:
And Whereas the Ministry of Health and Local
Government for Northern Ireland (hereinafter re-
ferred to as "the Ministry") on the 30th August,
1963, made an Order confirming the said Order:
And Whereas by Section 7 of the Local Govern-
ment Act (Northern Ireland) 1922, it is enacted in
effect that the Ministry may make Orders dividing
an Urban District into wards and fixing the bound-
aries of such wards, altering the number and
boundaries of the wards or altering the boundaries

apportioning the Urban District councillors among
the wards:

Urban District Councillors were assigned to the said
Wards:
And Whereas it appears expedient to the Ministry
after consultation with the Council of the Urban Dis-
trict that in consequence of the alteration of the
boundary of the Urban District the boundaries of
the wards of the Urban District should be altered
without altering their number and that Urban District
Councillors should be apportioned thereto as herein-
after more particularly appears:
Now, Therefore, the Ministry, in exercise of the
powers vested in it by the Local Government Acts
(Northern Ireland) 1898 to 1962 and of all other
powers in that behalf enabling the Ministry, hereby
orders and directs as follows:
1. This Order may be cited as the Omagh Urban
District (Wards) Order 1963.
2. The Order made by the Ministry of Home Affairs
for Northern Ireland on the 25th day of June,
1935, dividing the Urban District into Wards and
apportioning the Urban District 
Councillors
among the said Wards is hereby revoked.
3. The Urban District shall be divided into three
Wards named respectively the North Ward, the
South Ward and the West Ward, and comprising
respectively the portions of the Urban District
described in column 2 of the Schedule, and the
Urban District Councillors shall be apportioned
among the Wards as set forth in column 3 of the
Schedule.
