Alteration of boundary of Urban
County District of Oungannon.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND LOCAL
GOVERNMENT FOR NORTHERN IRELAND
LOCAL GOVERNMENT ACTS (NORTHERN IRELAND),
1898 TO 1946
To .the Council of the administrative County of
Tyrone;
To the Council of the Urban County District of Dun-
gannon; and
To all others whom it may concern.
WHEREAS the Council of the Urban County Dis-
trict of Dungannon (in this Order referred to as "the
Urban District Council") on the 14th day of July,
1947, submitted a proposal to the Council of the
administrative County of Tyrone (in this Order re-
ferred to as "the County Council") for the alteration
of the boundary of the said Urban County District:
AND WHEREAS the County Council, in exercise
of the powers conferred upon them by section
twenty-four of the Local Government Act (Northern
Ireland), 1934, and of all other powers thereunto
them enabling, at a quarterly meeting held on the
15th day of December, 1947, made an Order (in
this Order referred to as "the Order") giving effect
to the said proposal with modifications:
AND WHEREAS the County Council have com-
plied with the provisions of the said Act and the
Urban Districts Boundaries (Procedure) Order, 1936,
as to the giving of notice of the provisions of the
Order:
AND WHEREAS the Urban District Council
within one month after the prescribed notice of the
Order appealed to the Ministry of Health and Local
Government for Northern Ireland (in this Order re-
ferred to as "the Ministry") against the Order:
AND WHEREAS it is enacted in effect by sub-
section (3) of section twenty-four of the said Act
that the Ministry shall hear and determine such
appeal and may make such Order in relation to the
appeal as the Ministry thinks fit, including an Order
confirming the proposal contained in the Order of
the County Council or an Order giving effect to
such other proposal in relation to the boundary of
the urban county district as the Ministry may deem
expedient:
NOW, THEREFORE, the Ministry, in exercise
of the powers conferred upon it by section twenty-
four of the Local Government Act (Northern Ire-
land), 1934, and of every other power in this behalf,
hereby orders and declares as follows, that is to
say:—
1. That the boundary of the urban county district
of Dungannon be altered by including within the
said urban county district the area described in
the Schedule hereto and more particularly delin-
eated on the map annexed hereto and thereon
coloured PINK and if there be any discrepancy
between the map and the description of the area
in the aforesaid Schedule the map shall prevail.
2. That every person holding office on the first day
of April, 1949, as county councillor or district
councillor for any area affected by this Order
and every county councillor or district councillor
elected for any such area before the first day
of April, 1949, shall be deemed to have been
elected for the area as altered by this Order, and
the county council or district council of which he
is a member shall be deemed to have been elected
for the county or district as altered by this Order.
3. That the bye-laws at present in force in the said
urban county district shall apply to the area of
the said urban county district as altered by this
Order.
4. That the local and adopted Acts and local Orders
in force in the said urban county district shall
apply to the area of the said urban county dis-
trict as altered by this Order.
5. That 
notwithstanding the 
alteration of the
boundary of the said urban county district by
this Order the County Council shall have power
to recover arrears of rates in the area affected.
6. That the financial adjustments consequent upon
the making of this Order shall be the subject of
an agreement to be entered into by the parties
7.
8.
concerned, such agreement to be approved by the
Ministry, and in the absence of agreement be-
tween the parties to be determined by the
Ministry.
That the said altered area do form the said
urban county district of Dungannon for all pur-
poses as from the 1st day of April, 1949.
That this Order may be cited as the Dungannon
Urban District (Alteration) Order, 1948.
SEALED with the Official Seal of
the Ministry of Health and Local
Government for Northern Ireland this
26th day of August, one thousand,
nine hundred and forty-eight, in the
presence of
