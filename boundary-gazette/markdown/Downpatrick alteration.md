ORDER
Of the Council of the Administrative County of
Down altering the Boundary of the said
Urban County District
Whereas on the 23rd day of September, 1965,
application was made by the Urban County District
of Downpatrick to the Council of the Administrative
County of Down (hereinafter called the County
Council) pursuant to the provisions of Section 24 of
the Local Government Act' (N.T.) 1934, for the
alteration of the existing Boundary of the said Urban
County District.
And whereas the County Council being satisfied
that a prima facie case had been made out, caused a
Local Inquiry to be held on the 15th day of Feb-
ruary, 1966, by a Committee consisting of G. McSpad-
den, Esq., O.B.E., LL.B., F. C. Tughan, Esq., and
Captain D. J. R. Ker, M.A., D.L. (hereinafter called
the Committee).
And whereas the Committee duly reported its
findings to the Finance, Law and Parliamentary Com-
mittee of the County Council on the 22nd day of
April, 1966.
And whereas the said Finance, Law and Parlia-
mentary Committee recommended 
to the County
Council that effect be given to the application of the
Urban County District of Downpatrick subject to
certain modifications.
And whereas at the Meeting of the County Council
held on the 5th day of May, 1966, the said County
Council accepted the recommendations of the Fin-
ance, Law and Parliamentary Committee of the said
County Council.
Now therefore, we the Council of the Adminis-
trative County of Down, in pursuance of the powers
conferred upon us by the said Section 24 of the
Local Government Act (N.I.) 1934, and of all other
powers us thereunto enabling, do hereby order as
follows:
(1) That the Boundary of the said Urban County
District of Downpatrick be altered by includ-
ing within the said Urban County District of
Downpatrick 
the 
area 
described 
in 
the
Schedule hereto and more particularly deline-
ated on the Map annexed hereto, whereon
the additional area is shown between the red
line and the green line.
(2) That every person holding office on 1st April,
1966, as County or District Councillor for any
area affected by this Order and every County
or District Councillor elected for any such
area before the 1st day of April, 1966, shall
be deemed to have been elected for the area
as altered by this Order, and the County or
District Council of which he is a member
shall be deemed to have been elected for the
County District as altered by this Order.
(3) That the Bye-Laws at present in force in the
said Urban County District shall apply to the
area added to the said Urban County District
by this Order.
(4) That the local and adopted Acts and local
Orders in force in the said Urban County
District shall apply to the area added to the
said Urban County District by this Order.
(5) That notwithstanding the alteration of the
Boundary of the said Urban County District
by this Order the said County Council shall
have power to recover arrears of rates in the
area affected.
(6) That the financial adjustments 
consequent
upon the making of this Order shall be the
subject of an Agreement to be entered into
by the parties concerned, such Agreement to
be approved by the Ministry of Development
for Northern Ireland, and in the absence of
agreement between the parties shall be deter-
mined by the said Ministry.
(7) Save as hereinafter expressly provided this
Order shall come into operation on the 1st
day of April, 1967.
Provided that for the purposes of:
(a) the alteration or re-arrangement of any
register of electors;
(b) the alteration of valuation lists to take
part on the appointed day or the pre-
paration or service of any precept to be
made in respect of the financial year
commencing on the appointed day; and
(c) the preparation or approval of any general
rate or special rate to be made in respect
of a period commencing on the appoin-
ted day;
this Order shall operate from the confirmation
thereof by the Ministry of Development.
(8) This Order may be cited as the Downpatrick
Urban District (Alteration) Order, 1966.
Sealed with the Common Seal of the
Council of the Administrative County of
Down at a meeting thereof held this
16th day of June, 1966.
(Signed) Wm. J. McCoubrey, Chairman.
(Signed) /. C. Pantridge, Secretary.
