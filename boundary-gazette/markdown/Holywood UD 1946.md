To the Council of the administrative County of
Down;
To the Council of the Urban County District of
Holy wood; and
To all others whom it may concern.
Whereas the Council of the. Urban County District
of Holywood. (in this Order referred to as " the
Urban District Council") on the 5th day of October,
1944, submitted a proposal to the Council of the
administrative County o£ Down (in this Order re-
ferred to as the "County Council1") for the altera-
tion of the boundary of the said Urban County
District:
And whereas the County Council, in exercise of
the powers conferred upon them by section twenty-
Jour of the Local Government Act (Northern Ire-
land), 1934, and of all other powers thereunto them
enabling, at a' half-yearly meeting held on the 3rd
day of May, 1945. made an Order (in this Order
referred to as " the Order ") giving effect to the said
proposal with modifications:
And whereas the County Council have complied
with the provisions of the said Act and the Urban
Districts Boundaries (Procedure) Ordef, 1936, as to
the giving of notice of the provisions of the Order:
And whereas the Urban District Council within
one month after the prescribed notice of the Order
appealed to the Ministry of Health and Local
Government for Northern Ireland (in this Order
referred to as "the Ministry") against the Order:
And whereas it is enacted in effect by sub-section
(3) of section twenty-four of the said Act that the
Ministry shall hear and determine such appeal and
may make such order in relation to the appeal as
the Ministry thinks fit, including an order confirming
the proposal contained in the order of the county
council or an order giving effect to such other pro-
posal in relation to the boundary of the urban
county district as the Ministry may deem expedient:
Now therefore, the Ministry, in exercise of the
powers conferred upon it by section twenty-four of
the Local Government Act (Northern Ireland), 1934,
and of every other power in this behalf, hereby
orders and declares as follows, that is to say:—
1. 
That the boundary of the Urban County Dis-
trict of Holywood be altered and extended by
including within the said Urban County District
the area described in the Schedule hereto and
more particularly delineated on the map annexed
hereto and thereon coloured brown.
2 
That every person holding office on the first day
of April, 1946, as county councillor, district
councillor or guardian for any area affected by
this Order and every county councillor, district
councillor or guardian elected for any such area
before the first day of April, 1946, shall be
deemed to have been elected for the area as
altered by this Order, and the county council,
district council or board of guardians of which
he is a member shall be dteemed to have been
elected for the county district or union as
altered by this Order.
3. 
That the bye-laws at present in force in the said
Urban County District shall apply to the area
added to the said Urban County District by this
Order.
4. 
That the local and adopted Acts and local
Orders in force in the said Urban County Dis-
trict shall apply to the area added to the said
Urban County District by this Order.
5. 
That notwithstanding the alteration of the boun-
dary of the said Urban County District by this
Order the County Council shall have power to
recover arrears of rates in the area affected.
6. That the financial adjustments consequent upon
the making of this Order shall be the subject of
an agreement to be entered into by the parties
concerned, such agreement to be approved by
the Ministry, and in the absence of agreement
between the parties to be determined by the
Ministry.
7. That the said extended area do forir part of
the said Urban County District of Holywpod for
all purposes as from the 1st day of April, 1946.
8. That the provisions of this Order shall not
prejudice or affect the powers and rights of the
