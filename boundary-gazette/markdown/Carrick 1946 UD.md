MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND LOCAL

LOCAL GOVERNMENT ACTS (NORTHERN
IRELAND). 1898 to 1944.
To the Council of the administrative County of
Antrim;
To the Council of the Urban County District of
Carrickfergus; and
To all others whom it may concern.
WHEREAS the Council of the Urban County District
of Carrickfergus (in this Order referred to as " the
Urban District Council"), on the 27th day of July,
1938, submitted a proposal to the Council of the.
administrative County of Antrim (in this Order re-
ferred to as " the County Council") for the altera-
tion of the boundary of the said Urban County
District:
AND WHEREAS the County Council, in exercise of
the powers conferred upon them by section twenty-
four of the Local Government Act (Northern Ire-
land), 1934, and of all other powers thereunto them
enabling, at a meeting held on the 25th day of April,
1939, made an Order (in this Order referred to as
"the Order") giving effect to the said proposal
.with modifications :
AND WHEREAS the County Council have complied
with the provisions of the said Act and the Urban
Districts Boundaries (Procedure) Order, 1936, as to
the giving of notice of the provisions of the Order :
AND WHEREAS the Urban District Council, within
one month after the prescribed notice of the Order,
appealed to the Ministry of Home Affairs for Nor-
thern Ireland against the Order :
AND WHEREAS the functions of the Ministry of
Home Affairs for Northern Ireland under the said
section are transferred to the Ministry of Health
and Local Government .for Northern Ireland (in
this Order referred to as "the. Ministry"):
AND WHEREAS it is enacted-in effect by sub-section
(3) of section twenty-four of the said Act that the
Ministry shall hear and determine such appeal and
may make such Order in relation to the appeal as
the Ministry thinks fit, including an Order con-
firming the proposal contained in the order of the
county council, or an order giving effect to such other
proposal in relation to the boundary of the urban
county district as the Ministry may deem expedient:
Now, therefore, the Ministry, in exercise of the
powers conferred upon it by section twenty-four of
the Local Government Act (Northern Ireland), 1934,
and of every other power in this behalf, hereby
orders and declares as follows, that is to say:—
1. That the boundary of the Urban County District
of Carrickfergus be altered and extended by in-
cluding within the said Urban County District
the area described in the Schedule hereto and
more particularly delineated on the map annexed
hereto and thereon coloured pink.
2. That the financial adjustments consequent upon
the making of this Order be the subject of an
agreement to be entered into by the parties con-
cerned, such agreement to be approved of by the
Ministry, 
and in the absence of agreement
between the parties to be determined by the
Ministry.
3. That every person holding office on the 1st day
of April, 1946, as county councillor, district
councillor, or guardian for any area affected by
this Order and every county councillor, district
councillor or guardian elected for any such area
before the 1st day of April, 1946, shall be deemed
to have been elected for the area as altered by
this Order, and the county council, district coun-
cil or board of guardians of which he is a
member shall be deemed to have been elected
for the county district or union as altered by this
Order.
4. That the bye-laws at present in force in the
said Urban County District shall apply to the
area added to the said Urban County District
by this Order.
5. That the local and adopted Acts and local
Orders in force in the said Urban County Dis-
trict shall apply to the area added to the said
Urban County District by this Order.
6. That notwithstanding the alteration 
of 
the
boundary of the said Urban County District by
this Order, the County Council shall have power
to recover arrears of rates in the area affected.
7. That the said extended area do .form part of
the said Urban County District for all purpose*
as from the 1st day of April, 1946.
8. 
That this Order may be cited as the Carrick-
fergus Urban District (Extension) Order, 1945.
SEALED with the Official Seal of the Ministry of
Health and Local Government for Northern
Ireland this Twentieth day of October, One
Thousand Nine Hundred and Forty-five, in the
presence of
G. W. BROWNELL,
Assistant Secretary.
