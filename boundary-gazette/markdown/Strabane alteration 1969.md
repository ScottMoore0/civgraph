PAY OF GROUND STAFF AT AERODROMES
Civil publicity-owned aerodromes in the 
United
Kingdom are at present operated either by the
British Airports Authority, local authorities, or the
Board of Trade. The pay structures devised by these
operating authorities for the ground staffs they em-
ploy on airport industrial duties differ widely and
this has led to difficulties at some aerodromes.
Accordingly, in pursuance of their powers under
section 2(1) of the Prices and Incomes Act 1966, the
First Secretary of State and Secretary of State for
Employment and Productivity, the Board of Trade,
the Secretary of State for Scotland and the Minister
of Housing and Local Government hereby refer to
the National Board for Prices and Incomes for ex-
amination the question-..of the pay and conditions of
service, and the principles for determining these, of
such ground staffs at civil publicly-owned aerodromes.
