Whereas on the 1st day of October, 1962, an applica-
tion was made by the Council of the said Urban
County District of Cookstown (hereinafter referred
to as the said "Urban Council") to the County
Council of the County of Tyrone (hereinafter called
the "County Council") pursuant to the provisions of
Section 24 of the Local Government Act (N.I.), 1934,
for the alteration and extension of the existing
boundary of the said Urban County District.
And Whereas the said County Council being satis-
fied that a prima facie case had been made out for
the proposal to alter the existing boundary of the
said Unban County District by extending same, and
pursuant to the said Act, and to the said Order, by
Resolution dated 12th day of November, 1962,
appointed W. H. Smith, Esq., LLjB., W. S. Rea, Esq.,
B.A., 
'MjB.E., and William (Purdy, Esq., being
members of the County Council, to hold such Local
Inquiry as authorised and prescribed by the said
Act and Order.
•And Whereas the said Messrs. Rea and Purdy held
such Local Inquiry (due notice of same and of the
purport thereof having been previously given in
compliance with the requirements of the said Order)
at the Urban Council Chambers, Cookstown, in the
said County, on Monday, 3rd day of December, 1962.
And Whereas the said Messrs. Rea and Purdy duly
made their report of such Inquiry to the County
Council at the Adjourned Quarterly 'Meeting thereof,
held on the 10th day of December, 1962.
And Whereas the County Council on consideration
of said Report at said meeting held on the 10th day
of December, 1962, did adopt the same and directed
that steps be taken to have an Order prepared and
made embodying the terms of said report for sub-
mission to the Ministry of Health and Local Govern-
ment for Northern Ireland.
Now, Therefore, We the County Council of the
said Administrative County of Tyrone in pursuance
of the powers conferred upon us by the said Section
24 of the Local Government Act (Northern Ireland),
1934, and all other powers us thereunto enabling Do
Hereby Order as follows:—
1. The adjustment or award made by J. W. Drury,
Esq., and bearing date the 26th day of September,
1901, and the Order of the County Council dated the
6th day of September, 1906, as approved by an
Order of the Local Government Board, dated 27th
day of March, 1907, and the Order of the County
Council dated llth September, 1939, as approved by
an Order cited as the Cookstown Urban District
(Alteration) Order, 1947, and dated 13th February,
1947, in so far as they determine the financial rela-
tions between the Administrative County of Tyrone
and the Urban County District of Cookstown shall
apply and have effect in like manner as if the Urban
County District of Cookstown therein mentioned
were the Urban County District as extended by this
Order when confirmed by the Ministry of Health and
Local Government.
2. That the boundary of the Urban County Dis-
trict of Cookstown be altered by including within the
said Urban County Distract the area described in the
schedule hereto and more particularly delineated on
the map annexed hereto and coloured pink.
3. That the financial adjustments consequent upon
the making of this Order shall be the subject of an
Agreement to be entered into by the parties con-
cerned, such agreement to be approved of by the
Ministry and in the absence of agreement between
.the parties to be determined by the Ministry.
4. That every person holding office on the 1st
day of April, 1963, as County Councillor or District
Councillor for any area affected by the Order, and
every County Councillor 
or District Councillor
elected for any such area before the 1st day of April,
T963, shall be deemed to have been elected for the
area as altered by the Order, and the County Council
or District Council, of which he is a member, shall
be deemed to have been elected for the County Dis-
trict as altered iby the Order.
5. That the Bye-Laws at present in force in the
said Urban County District shall apply to the area
as altered by the Order.
6. That the local and adopted Acts and local
Orders in force in the said Urban County District
shall apply to the area as altered by the Order.
7. That notwithstanding the alteration of the
boundary of the said Urban County District by the
Order, the said County Council shall have power to
recover arrears of rates in the area affected.
8. The said altered area do form the said Urban
County District of Cookstown for all purposes as
from the 1st day of April, 1963.
