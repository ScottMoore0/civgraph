# Welcome to Boundaries Website

The Boundaries Website is an interactive map explorer for **Northern Ireland** and **Ireland**, providing access to hundreds of boundary datasets spanning local government, electoral, historical, physical, and community boundaries.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Electoral boundaries** — Wards, District Electoral Areas, Parliamentary and Assembly constituencies
- **Local government** — Council districts from 1973 to present
- **Historical boundaries** — Counties, baronies, civil parishes, and townlands
- **Physical geography** — Watersheds, highlands, and natural features
- **Census and statistical areas** — Super Output Areas, Small Areas, and more

## How to Use

1. Browse the **Catalogue** on the left to find maps by category
2. Click any map to load it onto the interactive map
3. Use the **search bar** to find specific places, features, or addresses
4. Toggle multiple layers to compare boundaries across time periods
5. Use the **time slider** to see how boundaries have changed over the years

## Data Sources

Most boundary data comes from public sector sources available under the [Open Government Licence v3.0](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/), including OSNI, NISRA, and various boundary commissioners. Some layers have been digitised from historical source material.
