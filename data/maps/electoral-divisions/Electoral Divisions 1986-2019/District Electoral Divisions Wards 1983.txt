Name - District Electoral Divisions/Wards 1983

Provider - Phelim Birch, Paddy Matthews (Paddy will be credited on all my ED maps from now on as I used his 1926 map for Galway)

Year - 1983

Notes - Modified version of District Electoral Divisions/Wards 1986. In 1986 (following the expansion of Dublin county borough a year prior), Dublin’s existing 141 wards were replaced by 162 new ones and County Dublin’s existing 80 DEDs were replaced by 159 new ones. The new county borough of Galway was given 22 wards, replacing 4 of the existing County Galway DEDs (Galway West Urban, Galway East Urban, Galway North Urban, Galway South Urban) and reducing the size of 4 other County Galway DEDs (Barna, Galway Rural, Carrowbrowne, Ballintemple). The following sources were used to get boundaries for Dublin:
- County Borough of Dublin (Wards) Regulations, 1970
- Dublin County (District Electoral Divisions) Regulations, 1971
