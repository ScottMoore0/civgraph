Name - District Electoral Divisions/Wards 1986
Provider - Phelim Birch
Year - 1986
Notes - Modified version of District Electoral Divisions/Wards 1994. In 1994, the boundaries of 9 DEDs and 4 wards were amended following the minor border changes to Dublin county borough and the counties (formerly electoral counties) of Dún Laoghaire-Rathdown, Fingal and South Dublin. I used the following sources to get the old boundaries:
- Local Government (Reorganisation) Act, 1985
- Local Government in Dublin: A New Beginning: The Reorganisation Report (1992)
- Dublin City Development Plan 1991
- Dublin County Development Plan 1993
