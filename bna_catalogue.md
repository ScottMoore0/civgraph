# British Newspaper Archive — Statements of Persons Nominated (Northern Ireland, 1950–1988)

Catalogue of 68 newspaper clippings downloaded from the British Newspaper Archive, each containing a Statement of Persons Nominated (and/or Notice of Poll or Declaration of Result) for elections in Northern Ireland.

> [!NOTE]
> Election types have been identified from the article text and cross-referenced with known NI election dates. BL codes identify the newspaper title in the British Library catalogue. Dates shown are publication dates (the election itself may be on a different date). Some OCR snippets are garbled; constituency names have been reconstructed where possible.
